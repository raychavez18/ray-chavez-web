E-Commerce

Buying online has become a major keyrole in day to day life now. With major sites like Amazon.com, smaller retailers see that having an online store is crucial and a necessity to survive. I believe that getting the basics right of ecommerce with security, privacy and return policies goes a long way. Also, customer service is key to an ecommerce site. After a customer feels at home and secure, the next goal is to match people with the product and then checkout.

Responsive Web Design

Responsive Web Design is truly something else. Being someone that designs and codes HTML/CSS, I like to make the user expierence for the websites I create easy to use and web friendly. It is the design and development of a website that creates the first (and overall) impression of your business and services. I'm a firm believer in that web design is a key process that combines professionalism and implementation of the latest innovation and design techniques.

CMS Integration

A website is never finished, even after launch. Keeping content new and fresh is a vital part for any online business to stay alive in today's age. With all the Content Management Systems out there, users now have an easy platform to update their websites right from home. Wordpress is one of the most powerful content management systems today that I am very proficient in. Drupal, Web Publisher and Adobe CQ5 are just a handful of other CMS's I have knowledge in.
